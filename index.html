About Us

Empowering Entrepreneurs, Building Futures, we strive to create an inclusive ecosystem where individuals are equipped with the resources, knowledge, and support needed to turn their startup dreams into lasting realities.

Our Mission

  • Fostering a collaborative community for startup enthusiasts to learn, grow, and succeed together.
  • Providing a supportive network where entrepreneurs can find mentorship, resources, and opportunities.
  • Inspiring a culture of innovation, resilience, and continuous learning to drive entrepreneurial success.

At BuildATL, we firmly believe that entrepreneurship extends beyond the mere creation of businesses; it's about crafting futures brimming with promise and opportunity. Rooted in the core values of collaboration, unwavering support, and empowerment, BuildATL transcends the conventional notion of a mere community—it's a dynamic movement propelling individuals towards their entrepreneurial aspirations. Whether you're a seasoned veteran navigating the intricacies of the startup landscape or a newcomer embarking on your maiden voyage, we invite you to join us on this exhilarating journey. Together, let's embark on the adventure of transforming innovative concepts into tangible realities, one startup at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I join BuildATL?

    To join BuildATL, simply visit our website and be on the look out for our events.

  • What types of startups are welcome in BuildATL?

    BuildATL welcomes startups from diverse industries and backgrounds. Whether you're working on a tech innovation, a social enterprise, or a traditional brick-and-mortar business, our community is open to all passionate entrepreneurs with a drive to innovate and grow.

  • How do I find a mentor through BuildATL?

    Finding a mentor at BuildATL is easy. Once you're a member, you can explore our mentorship program and connect with experienced entrepreneurs who match your interests and needs. Our platform facilitates mentor-mentee connections, and you can request mentorship directly through our system.

  • What networking opportunities does BuildATL offer?

    BuildATL hosts a variety of networking events, including industry mixers, themed meetups, and online forums. These events provide opportunities for entrepreneurs to connect, collaborate, and build valuable relationships within our vibrant community.

  • Does BuildATL provide funding for startups?

    While BuildATL doesn't directly provide funding for startups, we offer guidance and support to help you navigate the funding landscape. Our team can assist you in refining your pitch, connecting with potential investors, and exploring various funding sources to secure the capital your startup needs.

  • What resources are available through BuildATL's platform?

    BuildATL provides access to a wide range of resources, including industry insights, expert workshops, funding opportunities, and tools for startup growth. Members can explore our resource hub to find valuable information, support, and guidance tailored to their entrepreneurial journey.
